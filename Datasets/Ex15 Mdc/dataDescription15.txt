 Tobacco Quitline Calls by Zip, Racial Demographic, and Pregnancy Group

This dataset contains the total number of calls to the tobacco quitline by Indiana zipcode, reported racial demographic, and whether the individual reported pregnant, breastfeeding, or planning to be pregnant.
